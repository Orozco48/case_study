Incorporated:

founders:  Levi Cooperman, Mike McDarment, and Jose Sawada.

The idea 
Idea Came when McDarment had a small designer’s company and started have in issues with inventory and accounting. So he decided to code his own accounting software, eventual he started offering his program and was able to get external investors. 

Funding:
The company started funding itself until 2014 were it raised investments up to $30 million by the Venture capital firm Oak Investment partners. today it has secured $80.75 million in a Series E round of funding, as well as $50 million in debt financing. the company is meant to be used by small business owners to help have control and record of income and expenses, also facilitate the development of charging and sending invoices. J.P. Morgan, Gaingels, BMO Technology & Innovation, Banking Group and Manulife also participated investment, along with platform partner and new backer Barclays. With the new capital injection, FreshBooks has now raised a total of more than $200 million in funding over its lifetime.
